AUTOMOBILE & AUTO COMPONENTS




  • Jharkhand benefits from a vibrant presence of India’s leading Automobile Industry giants like TATA motors since 1950’s
  • More than 800 auto ancillary and auto component units employing over 20,000 employees directly and over 50,000 employees indirectly present in Jamshedpur and Adityapur
  • Steel being a major raw material for production of automotives and auto-components, the expansion of steel plants in Jamshedpur & Bokaro is giving an impetus to Auto & Auto-components industry in the state
  • The state is an excellent base for automobile ancillaries, precision, electronically & computer operated machine tools and farm equipment industries etc.
  • Strong local demand for industrial vehicles and mobile equipment like dumpers, excavators, cranes, etc. by metal & mining industry giants like Hindustan Copper, Central Coalfields and Bharat Coking Coal makes it a preferred location for manufacturing of these vehicles and equipment


With the objective of making Jharkhand a leader in auto-components manufacturing in a fast developing Eastern Indian market, the State Government recently came up with the Automobile & Auto-Component Policy 2015. The policy primarily aims to:

  • Attract mega automobile projects in the state and promote new auto-cluster and strengthening existing clusters.
  • Encourage establishment of Tier I, II & III auto-component manufacturers to create direct & indirect employment opportunities and to promote local entrepreneurship
  • Identify and address the existing infrastructure gaps affecting the automobile and auto-components industry.
  • Augment the talent pool to meet skill requirements of this industry and to encourage PPP in skill development
