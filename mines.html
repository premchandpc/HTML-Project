MINES AND MINERALS






Jharkhand ranks 3rd in terms of mineral production in the country and holds 40% of India’s mineral wealth. It accounts for 27% Coal, 26% Iron Ore (Haematite), 27% Apatite rock Phosphate, 20% Cobalt and 18% Copper Ore resources of the country. The state is among the leading producers of steel too.Jharkhand, in a sense is the backbone of the ‘Make in India’ campaign of the Government of India.

projests proposed for the sector are:

  • Proposed Aluminium Park at Muri and Latehar.
  • Proposed Gems and Jewellery Park at Ranchi.
  • Proposed Ceramic Park at Dhanbad, Sahebganj and Deoghar.
  • Proposed Steel Park at Kharsawan and Jamshedpur.
